FAQ

Is this a real store?

No. This is a school project demo. No real payments are processed.


How does checkout work?

The checkout stores a fake order ID in localStorage and shows an order success message.


Are accounts real?

Accounts are simulated using localStorage on your device.


What promo codes work?

Try FRESH20, FRUITY10, SUMMER15, or ROAR20.
